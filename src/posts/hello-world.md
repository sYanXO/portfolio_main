This is the first post on this site. I've been meaning to write things down for a while — not for an audience, but for myself. Writing forces you to be precise about ideas that otherwise live as vague intuitions.

## What I'll write about

Mostly things I'm learning or building:

- **Systems programming**: Go internals, concurrency patterns, things I run into while building backend services.
- **Distributed systems**: Notes from papers I read, things that surprised me, failure modes that seem impossible until they happen.
- **Quantitative thinking**: Backtesting strategies, probability puzzles, anything at the intersection of math and real-world decisions.

## Why not just use Twitter?

Some ideas need more than 280 characters. And I want a place where I can revisit and refine what I've written, rather than letting it disappear into a feed.

This site is intentionally simple. No comments, no analytics, no newsletter signup. Just writing.
